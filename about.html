Climate Change Global Conference-2022

The network is organizing a continental conference in Nairobi Kenya for the 12 member countries. The conference topics are on climate change, ToP methods in Africa, Courage to Lead, institutional Sustainability and Fundraising. The conference is scheduled to be held from 22nd to 29th October 2022.
